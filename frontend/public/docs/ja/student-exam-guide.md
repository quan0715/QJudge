# Student Exam Guide

> Status: 2026-03-11 (Updated based on Student User Manual)

This guide helps students familiarize themselves with the QJudge system's exam process, including environment checks, anti-cheat mechanisms, and paper exam operations.

## 1. Basic Settings

### Account Login
- Visit the official website at [q-judge.com](https://q-judge.com).
- Click "Login" and select "Login with SSO".
- Use your **NYCU Portal** student/staff ID and password.

### Personalization
- Click the "User" icon in the top right to open the menu.
- **Theme**: Supports Light, Dark, and System modes.
- **Language**: Available in Traditional Chinese, English, Korean, and Japanese (Traditional Chinese or English is recommended).
- **Display Name**: You can further set your account information in the Settings page.

## 2. Participating in an Exam

1. **Enter Contest List**: Click the "Contests" button in the navigation bar.
2. **Select Exam**: Click the corresponding "Contest/Exam" card.
3. **Register/Sign Up**: Click the "Register" or "Sign Up" button.
   - **Private Contests**: Requires a contest password provided by the teacher.
   - **Anonymous Mode**: If enabled, you need to provide a nickname for the exam.
4. **Wait for Start**: After successful registration, wait for the exam time to arrive.

## 3. Pre-check Environment & Eligibility

Before entering an exam with anti-cheat mode enabled, the system will guide you through:

- **Step 1: Identity Check**: Verify your eligibility and submission records.
- **Step 2: Environment Detection**:
  - **Browser**: Only latest Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge allowed (Incognito mode recommended).
  - **Monitor Count**: Must be a single physical monitor (please disconnect external displays).
  - **Fullscreen Mode**: Answering must be done in fullscreen.
  - **Screen Sharing**: You must authorize sharing of the "Entire Screen".
- **Step 3: Read Exam Rules**: Confirm to formally enter the exam.

## 4. Strict Exam Mode & Anti-cheat Detection

During the exam, the system continuously monitors the following behaviors:

### Environment Checks
- **Fullscreen Monitoring**: If you exit fullscreen, the system will issue a warning and require restoration within a time limit.
- **Screen Sharing**: Disabling screen share is prohibited. If disconnected and not restored, your exam will be **automatically and forcibly submitted**.
- **Single Monitor Check**: Multi-window environments are prohibited.

### Violation Checks
- **Focus Loss**: Includes moving mouse out of window, tab switching, or switching applications.
- **Shortcut Restrictions**: Copy/Paste and common dangerous shortcuts are disabled.

> **Warning**: If the number of violations exceeds the limit set by the system, you will be **forcibly locked out** or **automatically submitted**, and you may lose your eligibility.

## 5. Online Paper Exam Operations

In the Paper Exam screen:

- **Answering**: Follow the exam content and enter text or select options in the answer area.
- **Display Mode Switching**:
  - **List View**: Displays all questions at once.
  - **Full View**: Displays one question at a time; use navigation buttons to move between questions.
- **Question Navigation**: Click the middle icon to collapse/expand the question list for quick jumping.
- **Final Confirmation**: The system will list unanswered questions. Click "Confirm Submission" once finished.

## 6. Supported Question Types

- **True/False**: Select "True" or "False".
- **Single Choice**: Select the most appropriate answer.
- **Multiple Choice**: Check all possible correct options.
- **Short Answer**: Enter a short response in the text area.
- **Essay**: Enter a detailed multi-line response.

## Suggestions

- Use a PC or laptop; tablets or phones are not recommended.
- Ensure a stable internet connection before starting.
- If system anomalies occur, contact the proctor immediately.
